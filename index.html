ABOUT ME

I am one passionate cake maker and feel incredibly lucky to do what I love the most.
I turned my hobby into a business in 2014 and since then,
I am making custom cakes, cupcakes, cakepops, french macarons
and much more for weddings
and any family or company events. As I like to challenge myself, no request is off limits. I keep up to
date with the latest trends and continuosly research the newest techniques and designs.
Each order at Cakeland is special, no matter how big or small the event is,
the goal is to make the most perfect centerpiece.
